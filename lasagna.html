Trail Mom's Weeknight Lasagna

A delectible slice of noodle cake awaiting mastication

A Universally Loved Favorite

My wife has been making lasgana since before we were married. Her mother taught her the basics and she has been tweaking, improving, evolving, and making the dish her own for 20+ years. Not one person who has eaten this delicacy has ever complained. One bite is a party in your mouth and everyone is coming!!!

The Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 12oz of Shredded Mozzorella
  • 1 16oz Container Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Box Lasagna Noods - Oven Ready
  • 1 Jar Marinara Sauce/1 Jar Alfredo
  • 16oz Whole Milk Cottage Cheese
  • 6oz Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 3 Gloves Garlic - Chopped/Pressed
  • Half Large Onion - Finely Diced
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt, Pepper, Tony C

The Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F
  2. Partially Brown (Par Cook) Ground Beef over medium-high heat
  3. Add onions
  4. Finish browning beef, add garlic approx a minute before pulling beef
  5. Drain beef/onions and return to pan
  6. Combine marinara and alfredo sauce to pan with beef/onions
  7. Add half a jar (from marinara/alfredo jars) of water
  8. Bring to boil over medium-high heat
  9. Kill heat
  10. Combine Ricotta, Cottage, 1/2 Mozzorella, 1/2 Parm and 2 eggs into a medium size bowl and mix.
  11. Lay one full ladel full of sauce in the bottom of a casserole dish
  12. Add a layer of lasagna noods
  13. Add a layer of cheese mix
  14. Start another layer with more sauce, then noods, and repeat until remaining meat sauce and noods fit in the dish. Top layer must end with sauce
  15. Add remaining mozzorella evenly across the top
  16. Cover with foil
  17. Bake for 1 hour at 375°
  18. Top with remaining Parm and let stand for 15minutes
